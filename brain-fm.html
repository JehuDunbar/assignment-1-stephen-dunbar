Patented Technology

Brain.fm holds patents on key processes for creating functional music, including technology to elicit strong neural phase locking-allowing populations of neurons to engage in various kinds of coordinated activity-and technology to remove distractions in sound.

This makes our music unique, purpose - built to steer you into a desired mental state. In otherwords, we've found new ways to create music that help you do what you need to do.

Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientist at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic institutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also runlarge scale behavioral test, measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiments always include a control condition ('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure out tech is what makes the difference.
